Our story

Built by people who
know travel

WKLY.travel was founded on a simple frustration: the travel industry has been selling the same packages for decades. Same destinations, same hotels, same tours — dressed up with new photography and called something different.

Our team has spent over fifteen years working across every part of the travel industry — wholesale distribution, trade partnerships, corporate travel management, and direct luxury bookings. We've built relationships with the properties and operators that actually matter. We know where the value is, and where it isn't.

WKLY is our answer. One carefully curated escape, once a week, arranged to a level that most travel agents can't match. Available for seven days only. Then we move on. No repeats, no catalogue, no predictable itineraries.
