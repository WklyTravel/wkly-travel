Seven days, one island

01
Day 1 · 20 Sep · Bali

Arrival — Nusa Dua

Your flight lands at Ngurah Rai International Airport. A private transfer collects you and delivers you directly to Ayodya Resort Bali on the shores of Nusa Dua — one of the island's most exclusive coastal enclaves. Check in, step onto your balcony, and let the warm Balinese air settle around you. The resort, the pool, and a welcome dinner are all waiting.

Day 2 · 21 Sep · Nusa Dua

Resort Day — Beach & Pool

A full day at the Ayodya. The resort's private beach, the lagoon pool, morning yoga — take it at whatever pace feels right. Nusa Dua's calm, reef-protected waters make for perfect swimming and paddleboarding. Breakfast, lunch and dinner are all included. This is what a proper first full day looks like: nothing to arrange, nowhere to be.

Private beachAll meals incl.Morning yoga
03
Day 3 · 22 Sep · Mount Batur

VW Volcano Safari & Hot Spring

Board a classic vintage Volkswagen jeep and head north to Mount Batur's otherworldly volcanic landscape — black lava fields, a steaming caldera, and jungle village roads. The experience ends at a natural hot spring where geothermal waters rise directly from the volcano below. Back at the resort by late afternoon. A completely different Bali from the beach.

VW Volcano Safari incl.Natural hot springAll transfers incl.
04
Day 4 · 23 Sep · Ubud

Ubud — Temples & Rice Terraces

Transfer to Ubud, Bali's cultural heart. A guided morning through the Tegalalang rice terraces, carved in perfect tiers up the hillside. Then Tirta Empul — the sacred water temple where locals have come to purify for a thousand years. Lunch in Ubud's market streets, then back to Nusa Dua before sunset. The island's spiritual centre, seen properly.

Tegalalang terracesTirta Empul TempleGuided tour incl.
05
Day 5 · 24 Sep · Mount Batur

Sunrise Hike — Mount Batur

A 2:30am departure. You reach the summit of Mount Batur as dawn breaks across the island — 360-degree views over the caldera, Lake Batur far below, Mount Agung on the horizon. Breakfast is served at the top, with the entire island spread out beneath you. Back at the resort by noon. This is the kind of morning you tell people about for years.

Summit sunriseBreakfast at the topGuided hike incl.

Day 6 · 25 Sep · Nusa Dua

Balinese Spa & At Leisure

A day with nowhere to be. Morning by the pool or on the beach. This afternoon: your two-hour Balinese spa treatment — a full luxury massage, aromatic body scrub, and a flower bath. This evening, dinner at Lagoona Restaurant on the beachfront. A last long look at the Indian Ocean before tomorrow.
