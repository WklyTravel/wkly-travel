Day by day

Seven days,
two worlds

01
Day 1 · 24 November · Cairns

Arrival — Cairns

Fly from your East Coast city direct to Cairns. Your private transfer meets you at arrivals and takes you to your 5-star hotel in the heart of the city. Check in, breathe in the tropical air, and let Cairns settle around you. Tonight is yours — the Esplanade, a waterfront dinner, an early night. Tomorrow the reef waits.

Private transfer5-star hotelBreakfast incl.
02
Day 2 · 25 November · Cairns

Cairns — At Leisure

Breakfast at the hotel. Today is entirely yours to explore Cairns and the region at your own pace. The city is the gateway to one of the world's seven natural wonders — the Great Barrier Reef. Options abound: reef day trips, rainforest walks, the night markets, kayaking the inlet. Your WKLY concierge can book anything you'd like.

At leisureBreakfast incl.Concierge on call
03
Day 3 · 26 November · Kuranda

The Kuranda Scenic Railway

Transfer from your hotel to the historic Cairns Railway Station. Board the Kuranda Scenic Railway — a journey through ancient rainforest, across the Barron Gorge, past fifteen hand-cut tunnels and dozens of bridges built over a century ago. Arrive in Kuranda village. Transfer back to Cairns. One of Australia's great rail experiences, unhurried.

Scenic RailwayAll transfers incl.Breakfast incl.
04
Day 4 · 27 November · Uluru

Cairns → Uluru

Transfer to Cairns Airport. Fly to Ayers Rock Airport. Shuttle to the resort — and your first view of Uluru. There is nothing that prepares you for it. Check into your 4.5-star hotel at Ayers Rock Resort. This evening: a sunset viewing of Uluru, changing colour through ochre to deep violet as the light falls. Dinner at the resort.

Flight incl.Shuttle incl.4.5-star resort
05
Day 5 · 28 November · Uluru

Field of Light Dinner

Breakfast, then a day exploring the walking tracks and cultural sites around Uluru at your own pace. As the sun begins to fall, transfer to Bruce Munro's Field of Light — 50,000 slender stems tipped with frosted glass spheres that glow across the desert floor. Dinner is served under an open sky with Uluru lit by the last of the day's light.

Field of Light incl.Dinner incl.Uluru views
06
Day 6 · 29 November · Uluru

The Red Centre — At Leisure

An early alarm is worth it — Uluru at sunrise is one of those moments you carry for life. Breakfast, then a free day to explore the full suite of activities Ayers Rock Resort includes: guided cultural walks, the Kata Tjuta Valley of the Winds, dot painting workshops, camel rides at sunset. Or simply: sit, watch, and let the desert do its work.

Free activities incl.Kata TjutaSunrise viewing

Day 7 · 30 November · Departure

Departure — Home

Final breakfast at the resort. Shuttle to Ayers Rock Airport. Flight home to your city. Your WKLY travel pack — a curated PDF with every booking reference, contact, restaurant recommendation and experience note from the trip — is waiting in your inbox. Next Monday, we find something new.
